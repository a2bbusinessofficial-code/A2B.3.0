    Fit criteria

    The list has to qualify first.

    The pilot works only when there are enough usable records, a valuable treatment to recover, and someone on your team ready to handle interested replies.

    ✓ Strong Fit
    • 300 or more approved old contacts (500+ preferred)
    • Old inquiries, missed consultations, no shows, lapsed clients
    • Records from the last 24 months preferred
    • One service group worth ~USD 750 or more
    • Same day handoff owner inside the clinic
    • Clear booking path and usable SMS or Email
    ✗ Not A Fit
    • No old records or under 100 old contacts
    • No usable phone or email
    • Mostly low ticket services
    • No appointment availability
    • No one available to handle interested replies
    • No permission or lawful basis to contact the list
    How we count results

    We count booking opportunities, not random replies.

    A result counts when the person:

    All interested replies
    • ✓came from your approved old list
    • ✓showed interest in the selected treatment
    • ✓is interested in a service worth around USD 750 or more
    • ✓confirmed they can take the next step
    • ✓gave or confirmed usable contact details
    • ✓was handed to your clinic with context
    Counted booking opportunity

    A2B does not guarantee collected treatment revenue. Your clinic still controls response speed, appointment availability, consultation quality, pricing, trust, and closing.
