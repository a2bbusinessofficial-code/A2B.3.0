Cookie Policy.

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies and similar technologies are used on the A2B AI Technologies website at a2b.services, who sets them, and how you can control them. For broader information on how we handle your personal data, see our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners. Some cookies are set directly by the website you visit (first-party cookies); others are set by third-party services embedded on the page.

Similar technologies include local storage and session storage — browser-based mechanisms that store data on your device without using cookie files.

2. First-Party Cookies and Storage

We do not set any first-party cookies. Our website code does not write to document.cookie, localStorage, or sessionStorage. No login sessions, preference data, or tracking identifiers are stored on your device by A2B directly.

3. Analytics — Plausible (No Cookies)

We use Plausible Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Plausible is specifically designed to be cookieless and privacy-first:

  • No cookies are set — ever.
  • No personal data is collected or stored.
  • No cross-site tracking or fingerprinting.
  • Your IP address is never stored; it is used only to derive a country-level estimate and then discarded.
  • All statistics are aggregated and anonymous.

Because Plausible sets no cookies and collects no personal data, it does not require a consent banner under GDPR or PECR. Plausible Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Cookies

The following third-party services used on our website may set cookies in your browser:

Calendly — Contact Page Only

Our Contact page embeds a Calendly scheduling widget. Calendly may set the following cookies when the widget loads:

Cookie Name Set By Duration Purpose
__cf_bm Cloudflare (via Calendly) 30 minutes Bot management and security — distinguishes humans from automated traffic.
calendly.consent Calendly Persistent Stores your GDPR consent preference within the Calendly widget.
landing_page Calendly Session Records the page you were on when you opened the booking widget.
_ga Google Analytics (via Calendly) 2 years Analytics — used by Calendly internally to measure widget usage.
_gid Google Analytics (via Calendly) 24 hours Analytics — distinguishes users within a 24-hour window.
ajs_anonymous_id Segment (via Calendly) 1 year Analytics — anonymous identifier used by Calendly's event tracking.

These cookies are only set when you visit our Contact page and the Calendly widget loads. You can manage or decline these cookies using Calendly's own GDPR consent banner displayed within the widget, or through your browser settings. Calendly Privacy Policy.

5. Services That Do Not Set Cookies

The following third-party services are also used on our website but do not set any cookies in your browser:

  • Supabase — our backend database (form submissions only). Contact form data is sent server-side; no client-side cookies or storage are written.
  • Web3Forms — form submission email delivery. Operates entirely server-side via a POST request.
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery via CSS. No JavaScript is involved; only a standard HTTP request is made to Google's servers, which may log your IP address.
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs/Cloudflare) — icon library delivered as a CSS file. No cookies set.

6. What We Do Not Use

We do not use any of the following on this website:

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies (no Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, LinkedIn Insight Tag, TikTok Pixel, or Twitter Pixel)
  • Behavioural tracking or session recording tools (no Hotjar, Microsoft Clarity)
  • Live chat or CRM widgets (no Intercom, HubSpot, Crisp)
  • Cross-site fingerprinting

7. How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. The links below take you to guidance for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of third-party widgets such as Calendly on our Contact page.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time as the services we use change. Material changes will be indicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
